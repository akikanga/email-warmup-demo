## Pro Tips for Each Template

### Template 1 (Cold Outreach)
‚úÖ **Personalize:** Reference specific platform features  
‚úÖ **Keep short:** 150-200 words max  
‚úÖ **Include demo link:** Visual proof beats words  
‚ùå **Don't:** Generic "I can help with email marketing" pitch  

### Template 2 (Follow-Up)
‚úÖ **Give them an out:** "If I'm not the right person, ignore this"  
‚úÖ **Acknowledge busy:** Shows empathy  
‚úÖ **Ask who to talk to:** Gets you a referral even if they're not interested  
‚ùå **Don't:** Sound desperate or pushy  

### Template 3 (LinkedIn Request)
‚úÖ **Short message:** LinkedIn has character limits  
‚úÖ **Reference email:** "I sent you a note" creates familiarity  
‚ùå **Don't:** Send generic connection request with no message  

### Template 4 (LinkedIn DM)
‚úÖ **Thank them first:** For accepting connection  
‚úÖ **Provide immediate value:** Share case study link  
‚úÖ **Soft close:** "Happy to share insights if useful"  
‚ùå **Don't:** Immediately ask for a call  

### Template 5 (Schedule Call)
‚úÖ **Offer 3 time slots:** Makes it easy to say yes  
‚úÖ **Set expectations:** Tell them what call will cover  
‚úÖ **Share portfolio:** So they're prepared  
‚ùå **Don't:** Say "What's your availability?" (puts burden on them)  

### Template 6 (Post-Demo)
‚úÖ **Send within 2 hours:** Strike while iron is hot  
‚úÖ **Recap key points:** Shows you listened  
‚úÖ **Clear next steps:** No ambiguity  
‚ùå **Don't:** Wait days to follow up  

### Template 7 (Proposal)
‚úÖ **Attach PDF:** Looks professional  
‚úÖ **Reiterate value:** Remind them what they're getting  
‚úÖ **Clear payment terms:** No surprises  
‚ùå **Don't:** Send just a link to Google Doc  

### Template 8 (Kickoff)
‚úÖ **Be specific:** Exact dates, exact deliverables  
‚úÖ **Set communication norms:** Prevents miscommunication  
‚úÖ **Get them to take action:** "I need X by Y"  
‚ùå **Don't:** Assume they know what to do next
